Instructions (Look, Cover, Write, Check)

  1. Look: Look carefully at the word on the screen.
  2. Cover: Click the "Cover Word" button. The word will disappear.
  3. Write: Type the word from memory in the box.
  4. Check: Click the "Check" button. The original word will reappear. Compare your spelling and see the feedback.
  5. Click "Next" to continue.
